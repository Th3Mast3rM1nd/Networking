<h2>Some Level Top domains:</h2>

| Level Top Domains | Description |
|-------------------|------------|
|***arpa*** | Used for DNS mapping|
|***com*** | Commercial |
|***edu*** | Educational |
|***Gov*** | Government |
|***Mil*** | Military |
|***net*** | Network support groups or ISPs |
|***org*** | Other organisations (normally charities)|
|***int*** | International Organisations |
